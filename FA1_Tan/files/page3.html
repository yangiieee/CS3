Lixue

  • Though Lixue isn't a main character like the other two characters I've mentioned, she still peaked my interest.
  • She was Fu Hua's Last student.
  • Her story is actually quite sad, to say the least.
    During her time under Fu Hua, she always strived to impress her, like she was her own mother.She just wanted Fu Hua to be proud of her.
  • Even in her last moments, Fu Hua was watching over her.
